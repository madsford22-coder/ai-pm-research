## Items

### Lenny Rachitsky - Why Your Product Stopped Growing: The 5-Step Framework
**Author:** Lenny Rachitsky (Former PM at Airbnb; Newsletter author)
**Source:** https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/why-your-product-stopped-growing

**tl;dr:** Lenny published 5-step framework for diagnosing why products stop growing, covering metrics, user behavior, competitive dynamics, and product execution patterns.

**What changed:** Published systematic framework for identifying root causes of growth plateaus.

**PM Takeaway:**
Growth plateaus require systematic diagnosis across metrics, user behavior, competition, and execution to identify root causes.

**PM problem addressed:**
PMs need frameworks to diagnose growth problems systematically rather than guessing at causes or applying generic solutions.

**How to apply:**
- Apply 5-step framework when growth plateaus: check metrics, analyze user behavior, assess competition
- Systematically eliminate potential causes before choosing solutions

**Decision this informs:**
How to diagnose growth problems, which growth levers to prioritize, and what data to examine when products plateau.

**Pattern to note:**
Product leaders sharing systematic diagnostic frameworks as PM craft evolves from intuition to methodical problem-solving.

### Vercel - AI Gateway Integration with Clawdbot
**Source:** https://vercel.com/changelog/use-ai-gateway-with-clawdbot

**tl;dr:** Vercel integrated Clawdbot with AI Gateway, enabling developers to add specialized AI capabilities through platform integrations.

**What changed:** Vercel added Clawdbot integration to AI Gateway platform.

**PM Takeaway:**
Platform integrations expand AI capabilities faster than building specialized features in-house.

**User problem impacted:**
Developers need specialized AI capabilities without building and maintaining custom integrations for each AI provider.

**Product surface area:**
Vercel AI Gateway - Clawdbot integration, multi-provider AI platform capabilities, and specialized AI feature access.

**Decision this informs:**
Whether to build specialized AI capabilities in-house versus integrating through platform providers, and how to expand AI feature sets through partnerships.

**Pattern to note:**
AI platforms expanding through provider integrations to offer specialized capabilities without building everything themselves.
