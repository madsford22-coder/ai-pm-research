**Author:** Lenny Rachitsky (Former PM at Airbnb; Newsletter author)

**tl;dr:** Lenny Rachitsky published a case study on replacing a sales team with 20 AI agents, providing concrete insights into agentic AI deployment in business functions.

**What changed:** Published detailed case study analyzing the replacement of a sales team with AI agents.

**PM Takeaway:**
AI agents can replace entire business functions, requiring different product decisions than human teams.

**PM problem addressed:**
PMs need concrete examples of how to structure AI agent deployments and what tradeoffs to expect.

**How to apply:**
- Evaluate which business functions can be replaced by agents
- Design agent workflows with different error handling than human processes

**Decision this informs:**
Whether to replace human teams with AI agents and how to structure agent-based business functions.

**Pattern to note:**
Companies systematically replacing human teams with AI agents, not just augmenting them.
